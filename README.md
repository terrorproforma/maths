# maths

Research notes, computational certificates, and reproducible manuscripts.

> **Provenance notice.** The AI research conversations behind this repository began as a
> shared conversation started by another person, and one construction was pasted in from an
> external public announcement. Not everything in this repository is the repository owner's
> work. Read [`PROVENANCE_AUDIT.md`](PROVENANCE_AUDIT.md) before citing, submitting, or
> announcing anything here. Candidate future research targets are catalogued in
> [`CONJECTURE_TARGETS.md`](CONJECTURE_TARGETS.md).

## Projects

- [`2026-rounding-counterexamples/`](2026-rounding-counterexamples/) — standalone
  manuscripts and exact certificates for two weighted-rounding results:
  the machine-dependent weighted-chairman counterexample (**owner's session**) and the
  DGG/Goemans cost-conjecture counterexample (**third-party origin — see the audit**).
- [`2026-dgg-and-chairman-counterexamples/`](2026-dgg-and-chairman-counterexamples/) — a
  later combined package of the same two results with a single merged manuscript. The
  combined paper mixes third-party-derived and owner's-session material and is **not a
  submission vehicle**; see the provenance note inside.
- [`subtree-shuffling-counterexample/`](subtree-shuffling-counterexample/) — the
  fixed-parameter (d,p) = (7,15) obstruction to the subtree-shuffling conjecture
  (**owner's session**, but built from a third-party starting map — see its
  `PROVENANCE.md`).
- [`conversation-exports/`](conversation-exports/) — visible-transcript record of the
  originating conversation thread.

Every project carries its own status, provenance, review checklist, tests, and build
instructions. All mathematical claims are AI-generated and unverified until independently
audited.
